About Me

Hello, my name is Jared Waltisperger and I am an Information Technology graduate from Arizona State University. I am a spouse, father to two children, and a friend to many.

From the multitudes of professional paths in the IT field, I was naturally drawn to software development. I truly enjoy problem-solving, and programming provides an endless sandbox of creative solutions to solve any real-world problem. I am self-motivated and very committed to my career which allows me to work alone well, but I prefer to work with others. My fiancé often calls me a "social butterfly", and I would have to agree with her. I feel that I am in higher spirits and more energized when I am interacting with others.

I have many hobbies and interests. I enjoy working out at the gym or doing outside activities several times a week. I believe a healthy body leads to a healthy mind. I have learned how to blacksmith and even crafted my own wooden boat, powered by a weed eater motor. My interest in computers started at a young age when the dial-up tone caused excitement and Everquest was the best game I had ever played. During my youth, several computers met their demise in my pursuit of modifying and tinkering to push the computer to greater limits. Those failures eventually led to enough experience to build and maintain my custom-built computers for the past 15 years.
